Agent Scorecard delegation policy

Decide how much autonomy the agent gets next.

This static simulator turns the committed public sample summary into a policy decision: give more permissions, keep supervised, or stop delegation until the failure mode is fixed.

Give more permissions Portfolio average is at least 85/100 and the weakest trace is also at least 85/100.
Keep supervised Portfolio average is at least 70/100 and every trace is at least 50/100.
Stop delegation until fixed Any trace below 50/100 blocks autonomy, even when the average looks healthy.
